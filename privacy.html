Privacy Policy

Data Collection

EYEON DATA SURVEILLANCE SYSTEMS collects only the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our services. This may include system metadata, access logs, and security-related information.

Data Usage

Collected data is used exclusively for security monitoring, threat detection, and system improvement purposes. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Protection

All data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption both in transit and at rest. Access to sensitive data is strictly limited to authorized personnel.

User Rights

Users have the right to request access to their data, request corrections, or request deletion of their personal information, subject to legal requirements for data retention.

Compliance

Our systems and policies are designed to comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other major privacy regulations.
